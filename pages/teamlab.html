Day 5

teamLab

Step inside the art

Immersive digital-art museums where projected light, water, and mirrors react to you as you move through them. There's no clear line between you and the art, and it's like nothing else in the city.

Things to do

01Borderless

Artworks wander between rooms with no map and no route, you just get lost in it.

02Planets

You go barefoot and wade through water rooms and soft light, a full-body version.

03Lamp Forest

A mirrored room of hanging lamps that light up in a chain around you.

04Floral projections

Flowers bloom and animals form and scatter across the walls and floor in real time.

Good to know

  • Book a timed ticket well ahead, it sells out.
  • At Planets you wade through water, so wear or bring shorts.
  • Give it two to three hours, it's bigger than it looks.
