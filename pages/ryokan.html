Day 5

Ryokan

A traditional inn

A ryokan is a traditional Japanese inn and one of the best nights you'll spend in the country: a tatami room, a robe, a multi-course dinner served just for you, and hot spring baths down the hall.

Things to do

01Tatami room

Woven-mat floors, low table, and a view. Slippers off at the door.

02Yukata robe

You change into a light cotton robe and wear it to dinner and the baths.

03Kaiseki dinner

A long, seasonal multi-course meal, often served in your room. The highlight of the stay.

04Private & public baths

Onsen baths to soak in before dinner and again before bed.

05Omotenashi

The quiet, anticipatory hospitality, your futon is laid out while you eat.

Good to know

  • Dinner is usually included and is the main event, arrive by late afternoon for it.
  • Bathe before dinner, then soak again before sleep.
  • It's a splurge night, worth building the trip around one.
