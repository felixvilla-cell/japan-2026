Day 2

Shibuya

The Tokyo from the movies

The scramble crossing, the loyal dog, the neon and the crowds, this is the Tokyo you've seen on screen. When the lights change, a thousand people cross at once from every direction.

Things to do

01Scramble Crossing

The world's busiest crossing. Cross it, then watch it from above with a coffee.

02Hachiko statue

The bronze dog who waited years for his owner, Tokyo's favorite meeting spot.

03Shibuya Sky

An open-air rooftop deck 230m up, the best view straight down onto the crossing.

04Center-gai

The pedestrian spine of shops, arcades, and cheap eats behind the crossing.

05Nonbei Yokocho

Drinker's Alley, a tiny lane of postage-stamp bars for later in the night.

Good to know

  • The Starbucks and Shibuya Sky both give a great crossing view from above.
  • Nights are when it really glows.
  • Shibuya Sky sells timed tickets, sunset slots go first.
