Day 5

The Shinkansen

The bullet train south

The bullet train, 320 km/h and Kyoto in a little over two hours, smooth and silent and always on time. It's not just transport, the ride itself is one of the best parts of the trip.

Things to do

01Tokyo to Kyoto

The Tokaido line runs it in about 2h15, city center to city center, no airport hassle.

02The Fuji side

Reserve a seat on the right heading west and watch Mt. Fuji roll past the window.

03Ekiben

Beautiful boxed lunches sold on the platform, eating one on board is part of the ritual.

04Nozomi vs Hikari

Nozomi is fastest, Hikari is covered by the rail pass, both get you there in comfort.

Good to know

  • Sit on the right side going west for the Mt. Fuji view.
  • Grab an ekiben and a coffee before you board.
  • Be on the platform early, it leaves to the second.
