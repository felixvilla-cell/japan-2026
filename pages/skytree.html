Day 2

Tokyo Skytree

634 meters over Tokyo

The tallest tower in the world at 634 meters, and the futuristic bookend to a morning at a 7th-century temple. Two decks, a glass floor, and Mt. Fuji floating on the horizon on a clear day.

Things to do

01Tembo Deck

The main observation deck at 350m, with a glass floor panel to stand over the drop.

02Tembo Galleria

A spiraling glass skywalk climbing to 450m, the highest point you can reach.

03Sunset timing

Go up an hour before sunset and watch the city switch from gold to a sea of lights.

04Solamachi

The mall at the base, with an aquarium, food, and souvenirs if the weather turns.

Good to know

  • Book a timed ticket to skip the long line.
  • The air is clearest in the morning or right after rain, best for the Fuji view.
  • Sunset is the prime slot and sells out first.
