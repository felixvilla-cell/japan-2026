Day 3

Asakusa

Old-Tokyo streets

The traditional downtown around Senso-ji, where Tokyo still feels like the old city. Rickshaws, kimono rentals, retro sweets, and low streets you can just wander.

Things to do

01Nakamise-dori

The centuries-old shopping street, fresh rice crackers and warm bean cakes.

02Kappabashi Street

Kitchen-tool row, where restaurants buy their gear and the famous plastic food samples.

03Sumida River cruise

Hop a boat down the river toward the bay for a different view of the city.

04Hoppy-dori

A lively alley of cheap eats and drinks, great for an afternoon snack crawl.

05Rickshaw ride

A local puller will run you through the backstreets and point out the history.

Good to know

  • Pair it with Senso-ji, they're the same square.
  • Kimono rental shops are everywhere if you want the full look.
  • Look for the golden Asahi flame building across the river.
