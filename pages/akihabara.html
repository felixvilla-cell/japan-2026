秋葉原
Day 4

Akihabara

Electric Town

Whole buildings that are nothing but arcades, seven or eight floors each, wrapped in anime billboards and electronics towers stacked to the ceiling. The one Felix will go crazy for.

Things to do

01Multi-floor arcades

GiGO and Taito Stations, floors of claw machines, fighting games, and rhythm machines.

02Super Potato

The legendary retro-game store, old Nintendo and Sega you can't find anywhere else.

03Yodobashi Akiba

A giant of a electronics store, nine floors of everything with a plug.

04Anime megastores

Animate and Mandarake for manga, figures, and collectibles.

05Gachapon halls

Rooms wall-to-wall with capsule-toy machines, a few hundred yen a pull.

Good to know

  • Budget a big chunk of the day, it's easy to lose hours here.
  • Weekends close the main street to cars, so you can walk it freely.
  • Bring coins for the arcades and gachapon.
