清水寺
Day 6

Kiyomizu-dera

The temple on stilts

A wooden temple hall jutting off a hillside on massive pillars, built without a single nail, with all of Kyoto spread out below. The walk up through the preserved lanes of Higashiyama is half the experience.

Things to do

01The main stage

The famous veranda hanging 13 meters over the hillside, the classic Kyoto view.

02Otowa Waterfall

Three streams below the hall, drink from one for luck in studies, love or long life. Pick one, taking all three is greedy.

03Sannenzaka & Ninenzaka

The two sloping stone lanes below the temple, wooden shops, tea houses and zero cars.

04Yasaka Pagoda

The five-story pagoda rising out of the rooftops, Kyoto's most photographed skyline.

05Jishu Shrine

The matchmaking shrine on the temple grounds, walk between the love stones with your eyes closed.

Good to know

  • Morning light is best on the main hall, afternoons get packed.
  • The lanes below are full of snack stops, save room.
  • It flows perfectly into an afternoon wander toward Gion.
