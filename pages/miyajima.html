Day 12

Miyajima

The floating torii

A short ferry from Hiroshima, the island of Itsukushima Shrine and its great vermilion torii standing in the sea. Deer wander the streets, oysters grill on the waterfront, and at high tide the gate appears to float.

Things to do

01The great torii

Sixteen meters of camphor wood in the water, one of the classic views of Japan. Check the tide chart, high tide floats it, low tide lets you walk to it.

02Itsukushima Shrine

The shrine itself stands on stilts over the bay, corridors over the water.

03Island deer

Tame, everywhere, and shameless about paper and snacks. Guard the map.

04Grilled oysters & momiji manju

Hiroshima oysters off the grill, and warm maple-leaf cakes for the ferry back.

05Mount Misen ropeway

If legs allow, a cable car and short hike to a summit view over the Inland Sea.

Good to know

  • The ferry from Miyajimaguchi runs constantly and takes 10 minutes.
  • Sunset behind the torii is the closer, time the return loosely.
  • JR pass or IC card covers the JR ferry.
