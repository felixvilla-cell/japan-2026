Day 14

Hiroshima

The peace day trip

A shinkansen day trip west: the Peace Memorial and its park in the morning, Hiroshima-style okonomiyaki for lunch, and the island shrine of Miyajima in the afternoon. Heavy, beautiful and the day people most often call the best of their trip.

Things to do

01A-Bomb Dome

The skeletal dome left standing since 1945, preserved exactly, across the river from the park.

02Peace Memorial Museum

Moving and unflinching. Worth doing properly with a teenager, allow 90 minutes.

03Children's Peace Monument

Sadako and the thousand paper cranes, cases of folded cranes sent from kids worldwide.

04Okonomiyaki, Hiroshima-style

Layered with noodles instead of mixed, locals insist it beats Osaka's. Order at a counter griddle in Okonomimura.

Good to know

  • Osaka to Hiroshima is about 90 minutes on the Nozomi, go early.
  • Do the museum first, the island after, the day needs that order.
  • This day is optional by design, if Osaka has you tired, it flexes out.
