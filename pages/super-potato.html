Day 3

Super Potato

Retro-game paradise

The most famous retro-game store in the world, tucked into a few floors in Akihabara. Shelves of Famicom, Super Famicom, and imports you thought were long gone, plus a retro arcade up top.

Things to do

01Classic consoles

Famicom, Super Famicom, Mega Drive and more, boxed carts stacked floor to ceiling.

02Rare imports

Hard-to-find titles and collector editions, the reason people fly in for this store.

03Retro arcade floor

The top floor is a working arcade of old cabinets, coins in the slot.

04Game merch

Soundtracks, plush, and pixel-art souvenirs from the golden age.

Good to know

  • Prices climb for boxed and rare, browse before you buy.
  • The upstairs arcade is a great break from shopping.
  • It's cramped and busy, go with a mission in mind.
