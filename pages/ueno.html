Day 3

Ueno

Museums & the big park

A leafy park that holds Japan's greatest museums, a famous zoo, and a buzzing market street right beside it. The calm, cultural flip side to a morning in the arcades.

Things to do

01Tokyo National Museum

The largest and oldest museum in Japan, the country's art and history in one place.

02Nature & Science Museum

Dinosaurs, a blue-whale model, and hands-on science, great with a teen.

03Ueno Zoo

Japan's oldest zoo, and home to the giant pandas.

04Shinobazu Pond

A lotus-covered pond with a shrine on an island in the middle.

05Ameyoko Market

A loud, cheap open-air market street of food, snacks, and bargains under the tracks.

Good to know

  • Pick one or two museums, you can't do them all.
  • Ameyoko is the spot for a cheap, lively bite.
  • The park is big and shaded, a good midday breather.
